The Power of Semantic HTML

Posted by Jane Doe on August 9, 2025

What is Semantic HTML?

Semantic HTML means using tags that describe the meaning of the content they contain. This approach is fundamental for building accessible and maintainable websites.

Why It Matters for Accessibility

Assistive technologies, like screen readers, rely on semantic tags to interpret the structure of a page. They allow users to navigate by landmarks (like the `main` or `nav` section) and jump between headings, making the content usable for people with disabilities.
